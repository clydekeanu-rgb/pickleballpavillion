Built for Players, Not Just Games

The Pickleball Pavilion is Cebu's home for pickleball — six indoor courts, two outdoor courts, and a community that plays hard and hangs out harder.

Whether you're picking up a paddle for the first time or grinding through league season, we've built a space designed around one thing: getting you on the court faster.

No more group chats juggling schedules or guessing if a court's free. Book online, see real-time availability, and show up ready to play.
